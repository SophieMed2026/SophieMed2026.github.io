I chose this environment because it was my old desk in my dorm room. It was about the only thing I loved in my dorm room: my desk setup and my window. I thought it would be simple since it had standard shapes and not many details. I loved the shapes tool for the plants and background. The eraser tool also came in handy for making holes in the Swiss cheese plant and for cleaning up edges. I used different layers depending on the ground I needed. (foreground, background, and middleground). I like how After Effects gave it a simpler yet still chaotic view of the desk and window.
